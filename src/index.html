Who We Are

We are a team of passionate software consultants with a consistent track record of delivering solutions and advising clients on their most complex technical challenges.

Masterpoint is a software consultancy specializing in a select set of services that include Cloud architecture, DevOps automation, application development, and technical auditing. Our project successes have included short-term engagements and larger projects for diverse clients ranging from seed-funded startups, Fortune 20 enterprises, and many that fall in-between.

Masterpoint was founded by Matt Gowie, a technical manager, architect, engineer and current digital nomad originally from Philadelphia. Matt leads Masterpoint’s remote team of expert consultants on all projects, with each team member selected for projects based on skills and specialization. Since Masterpoint’s founding in 2016, Matt has assembled project teams from his deep network of engineers, designers, and specialists.

What We Do

Our small teams provide on-demand expertise to help clients build solutions and drive sustainable improvements for future development efforts.

Masterpoint’s services are provided by a remote team of specialists carefully assembled to address the exact requirements of our clients, with on-site visits based on need. Most Masterpoint engagements include one or more of the following services:

Cloud Solution Architecture

Our cloud architects analyze the needs of the organization, recommend solutions appropriate for a client’s budget and technical requirements, and implement and configure architecture to ensure reliability and scalability for the business needs of both today and tomorrow.

Software Development

Whether our clients require full-lifecycle greenfield software development services or need a partner to rebuild, refactor, or maintain a legacy system, we have a track record of consistent on-time delivery.

DevOps and Automation

For clients that are seeking to improve the efficiency and integrity of development and deployment processes, our DevOps and automation specialists are experienced in designing and documenting process, helping clients navigate the ever-growing sea of automation tools, leading product and process implementations, and training teams in best practices to ensure success when our engagement is complete.

Architectural Audit

Our consultants work with stakeholders to understand their architecture needs and evaluate the existing cloud infrastructure to identify areas for improvement or missed opportunities for improved efficiency, performance, or cost savings.
